Activity 1: Career Expo Preparation Session

Date: March 15, 2025

Time: 2:10 PM - 3:00 PM

Location: Online

Category: Employer Expos/Fair

Evidence: Attendance Certificate, Zoom Screenshot

Activity Description

This preparatory session was designed to help students prepare for the upcoming Career Expo, a major networking event with potential employers.

The session provided comprehensive guidance on:

  • What to expect at the Career Expo, including layout, participating employers, and event schedule
  • Professional dress code recommendations for making a positive first impression
  • Appropriate behavior when interacting with potential employers
  • Preparation strategies, including research on companies and preparing elevator pitches
  • Introduction to the Career Expo mobile app for planning and scheduling interactions

What I Learned

From this preparation session, I gained valuable insights into professional networking in a career fair environment. Specifically, I learned:

  • The importance of researching companies before approaching their booths to demonstrate genuine interest
  • Proper business attire expectations and how appearance contributes to first impressions
  • Strategies for efficiently navigating the expo floor to maximise networking opportunities
  • How to use the Career Expo app to plan my visit, schedule appointments with recruiters, and keep track of connections

Relevance to My Professional Development

This preparation session was extremely valuable to my professional development as it equipped me with practical knowledge and skills for networking in formal professional settings. Understanding how to approach and engage with potential employers effectively is a crucial skill that will benefit me throughout my career.

The guidance on professional appearance and behavior helped me recognize the importance of non-verbal communication in professional settings.
